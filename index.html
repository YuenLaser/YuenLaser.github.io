Chiropractors Dr Benjamin Yuen and Dr Wilson Wong seek to provide you a better pain-free and healthy lifestyle through traditional chiropractic care without the use of drugs and surgery. We are also the largest laser clinic and teaching centre in Atlantic Canada. Laser therapy is the most advance medical treatment for pain. It eliminates the need for harmful medications and invasive surgery. Laser is most effective for back & neck pain, fractures, knee injury, arthritis, neuropathy, carpal tunnel, pinched nerve, concussion, sports injury, torn muscles, rotator cuff strain, skin lesions, TMJ pain, plantar fasciitis, open wounds and more. Our certified staff is caring, professional and competent. We are fluent in English, French, Spanish, Arabic and Chinese.
